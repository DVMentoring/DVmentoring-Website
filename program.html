Our Boarding Program

Our boarding house will open in August 2020 and will only have space to admit 20 Students into a special program design to be a four year progression project based group that will use the place-based and Harkness methods as well as indeirect leadership by Dr. Darren McKnight and his “Cognovation” ideological process and collaboration efforts to come up with projects they develop themselves with resources provide by the school and the backing of Two US based Engineering institutions we are currently in talks with and to be announced by Spring of 2020 with the aim of coming up with real solutions to solve local problems. This group will be selected based on a strict criteria and will primarily operate out of our new Rothenberger Innovations Building as their main hub. For more information on the program and how to apply, please send an email to Contact@DVSchool.com
